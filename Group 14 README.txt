Relations -

1. Customer & Reservations
   - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
   - One customer can make multiple reservations
   - Each reservation belongs to exactly one customer

2. Customer Representative & Schedules
   - Relationship: Many-to-Many (N:M)
   - Many customer representatives can modify many schedules
   - Each schedule can be modified by many representatives

3. Train & Schedules
   - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
   - One train can have multiple schedules
   - Each schedule belongs to exactly one train

4. Station & Schedule 
   - Relationship: Many-to-Many (N:M)
   - Many stations can be part of many schedules
   - Each schedule can include multiple stations

5. Station & Stops
   - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
   - One station can have multiple stops
   - Each stop belongs to exactly one station

6. Train Schedule & Reservations
   - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
   - One train schedule can have multiple reservations
   - Each reservation belongs to exactly one schedule

7. Reservation & Fare Discount
   - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
   - One reservation can have multiple fare discounts applied (child/senior/disabled)
   
8. Customer & FAQ
   - Relationship: Many-to-Many (N:M)
   - Many customers can interact with many FAQs
   - Each FAQ can be associated with many customers

9. Customer Representative & FAQ
   - Relationship: Many-to-Many (N:M)
   - Many representatives can handle many FAQs
   - Each FAQ can be handled by many representatives

10. Admin & Revenue
    - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
    - One admin can generate multiple revenue reports
    - Each revenue report is generated by one admin

11. Admin & Customer Representative
    - Relationship: One-to-Many (1:N)
    - One admin can manage multiple customer representatives
    - Each customer representative reports to one admin
